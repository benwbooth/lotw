# Legacy of the Wizard — full route (agent backbone)

Distilled from the **U Can Beat Video Games** narrated walkthrough
(`https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V9R8SGS5-LM`, "Retrospective + Ultimate Walkthrough", 1:21:43).

This is the **optimized** route (not the naive "each character gets their own crown" one).
Key facts that drive it:
- **Boss difficulty is fixed by crown COUNT**, not which crown: 1st crown → Tarantunes (easiest),
  2nd → Erebone, 3rd → Archwinger, 4th → Rockgaea (hardest), then Keela (final).
- **Power Fist** = 4× magic damage (but 2× magic cost) — essential vs every boss. **Pochi CANNOT equip it.**
- So the four crowns are collected by **Xemn, Lyll, Meyna, Xemn-again** (Xemn takes *Pochi's* area for the
  4th/hardest boss, using the Armor to pass Pochi-only enemies). **Pochi only does the opening item run.**
- Mechanics: kill enemies with <2 keys → they drop keys. **Inn** = 10 gold → near-full refill (use it freely).
  **Crystal** = warp to house (consumable). **Leaving a screen resets glove blocks** (recover from puzzle mistakes).
  **Pause then jump** escapes spike pits / cancels fall damage. **Die in the labyrinth → lose everything since the
  last house visit** (keep a password). The hub is "the dragon room" / crossroads — every run starts there.

Item-name map (manual names): Mattock(="matic"), Power Fist(="power fist"), Power Boots, Jump Shoes(="jump boots"),
Gloves, Rod (Meyna's block tool), Key Stick, Wings, Shield, Armor, Crystal, Elixir, Fire Rod, Crown, Dragon Slayer.

## Phase 0 — Pochi: opening item run (Gloves, Crystal)
Character **Pochi** (immune to enemy contact; weak/short attack; can stand on enemies).
1. From the house, go **right**, killing enemies to bank keys; grab the **money** chest. (Inn is just below — refill anytime.)
2. Jump off an enemy to the top brown platform; grab a **Scroll** (speed). Continue right, break a couple blocks.
3. Down here: collect the **Gloves** (Xemn-only block manipulation — push/slide/ride blocks). *Critical item.*
4. Head **back left**, down the left ladder → chest = **Crystal** (warp-to-house).
5. Continue right (avoid the rock-elemental and the 5-dmg **poison** drops; pause to negate fall damage), then
   **down-right** until the music changes → you've entered **Pochi's section**.

## Phase 1 — Pochi: get the Power Fist, then retire Pochi
1. Key the first door; head **left** across ladders past "Are-you" enemies (immune to magic — these gate the area).
2. Ride the orange **Brandy** enemy up, push through the **false wall at the top**, open the chest = **Power Fist**.
3. Path **up**, hold **left** to catch the ledge, grab a key, continue left. The far-left **tall ladder** returns to start.
   (Note the **woman's-head portraits** — once you have all 4 crowns they become warp points.)
4. Return to the **house**. **This is the last use of Pochi.** Choose **Xemn**; equip **Gloves + Power Fist**.

## Phase 2 — Xemn's area → CROWN 1 → boss **Tarantunes**
> Do Xemn's area first: it's the longest/hardest to traverse, so pair it with the *easiest* boss.
1. Go to the **dragon room** (hub). Glove-push the block left.
2. Up-right chest = the **Rod** (Meyna's block tool — needed later).
3. Enter **Xemn's area**; a key here gives **+10 keys**. Solve the glove puzzles (slide blocks by *jumping and tapping
   the direction right before landing*; **unequip the glove** before jumps you don't want to move a block).
4. **Power Boots**: a real chest hidden under a **fake-chest enemy** (kill it) — lets you stomp enemies without magic.
5. **Mattock** (Lyll's rock-breaker): **right above the shop**, an empty space behind a **false block**.
6. **Most dangerous spot:** push a block **down**, then **slide it RIGHT** (jump + right at the last instant). Sliding
   it left strands you → game reset. After it, push the block fully right and drop left.
7. Optional **Elixir** (auto-revive on death). Before the boss, equip **Elixir + Power Fist + Gloves**.
8. Climb the ladder to the top; the crown is to the **right** — cross **off the yellow floor**, avoid single-block holes.
   Grab **CROWN 1** → warp to **Tarantunes**: get close, switch to **Power Fist**, hit a few times — dies fast.

## Phase 3 — Lyll: fetch Wings, Fire Rod (note Dragon Slayer location)
Choose **Lyll** (highest jump, weakest attack); equip **Mattock + Power Boots + Crystal**. Hub → **Lyll's area**.
1. Mattock rocks left → chest = another **Crystal** (boost off the snake). Be careful of spikes (pause-jump out).
2. Right, mattock blocks, platform off skeletons. You'll pass the **Dragon Slayer** (get it only at the very end).
3. Down to the **volcano room**, go left, drop through the floor hole → **Wings** (Meyna's flight).
4. Boost off a slime left → **Fire Rod** (range; optional — only Pochi can equip it, otherwise prefer Power Fist).
5. **Crystal** → home.

## Phase 4 — buy the Shield (95 gold) + get the Key Stick
1. Farm **≥95 gold** in the hub (Power Boots on respawning enemies). The **Shield** can only be *bought*.
2. As **Lyll** (need Mattock), go **down-left into Meyna's area**. Break a **hidden block** to reveal a secret **inn**;
   **attack the inn sign** → it becomes a **shop** → buy the **Shield** (95g; blocks boss projectiles, vital vs bosses 3 & 4).
3. Continue into Meyna's area, down the ladder, left through a door. **Key Stick** is at the far left: the
   **next-to-last block is a secret hole** behind a false block (Meyna opens doors with magic instead of keys).
4. **Crystal** → home.

## Phase 5 — Meyna: Armor, Jump Shoes, spare Crystals (Meyna's area)
Choose **Meyna**; equip **Wings + Rod + Key Stick**. Hub → left, Rod the block, drop in.
> Wings drain magic fast — toggle off when not flying; Key Stick through doors to conserve keys.
1. Middle path through a door → spare **Crystal**.
2. Sneaky room: go far **left**, bottom tier, the **4th block is false** → drops to the **blue cave**. Touch the
   invisible **Mu** enemies before attacking.
3. **Armor** (makes Xemn invulnerable like Pochi, but drains magic — toggle): fly **far left** in this area.
4. Another **Crystal** up top (Key-Stick doors).
5. **Jump Shoes**: end of a moo-guarded door corridor; fly over the spikes, grab them, **Crystal out immediately**
   (don't walk back through the moos — they kill).

## Phase 6 — Lyll → CROWN 2 → boss **Erebone**
Choose **Lyll**; equip **Jump Shoes + Mattock + Power Boots**. Hub → **upper-right path** into Lyll's area.
1. Jump-shoes left for an **Elixir**; right past the Dragon Slayer; up into the gray/pink area; mattock the rocks.
2. Up the ladder, left; a **room full of rocks** — mattock through; invisible platforms / jump shoes near the end.
3. Before the crown, **inn**: equip **Power Fist + Mattock + Jump Shoes**. Tricky jump to the crown (pause-jump if missed).
4. **CROWN 2** → **Erebone**: close in, **Power Fist** mash (barely damages you on contact).

## Phase 7 — Meyna → CROWN 3 → boss **Archwinger**
Choose **Meyna**; standard **Wings + Rod + Key Stick**. Hub → Meyna's section (same route, going deeper; many inns —
one secret inn→shop sells cheap Elixirs/Magic potions).
1. Clear all the doors near the crown first. Before grabbing it, **inn**: equip **Shield + Power Fist + Wings**.
2. **CROWN 3** → **Archwinger** (hard): **Shield must be on** (it attacks before it's visible). Cross the top to a ledge,
   switch to **Power Fist** when it's close and mash; **switch back to Shield after the kill** — it keeps attacking as it
   dies and can still kill you (a tie goes to the boss → you lose the crown).

## Phase 8 — Xemn → CROWN 4 (Pochi's area) → boss **Rockgaea**
> Xemn takes Pochi's crown because the 4th boss is hardest and needs Power Fist, which Pochi can't equip.
Choose **Xemn**; equip **Armor + Gloves + Power Boots**. Hub → **Pochi's section**.
1. **Armor on** to walk through the Pochi-only immune enemies (toggle off after each, to save magic). Pochi's crown is
   quick to reach. Pause-jump off spikes; one glove puzzle (slide a block up-right as a stepping stone, or just hard-jump right).
2. Want **≥3–4 keys** here (keys are hard to get). Up-right **inn**; keep **Armor** (and Shield).
3. A **mimic chest** above deals **100 dmg** — pass it with **Armor**. Bounce off the enemy *while it's in circle form*
   (before it re-mimics) as a stepping stone.
4. **CROWN 4** → **Rockgaea** (hardest): **Shield on**, get into the corner, **Power Fist**, attack repeatedly;
   **re-equip Shield after the kill** (projectiles still lethal); never touch the boss body.

## Phase 9 — Roas: get the Dragon Slayer, beat Keela
1. Choose **Roas**; equip a **Crown + Crystal + Jump Boots**. Hub → **upper-right into Lyll's area** (the Dragon Slayer is here).
   Equip the **Crown** so jumping with Jump Boots doesn't burn magic; don't equip the Crystal where you'd trigger it by accident.
2. With the Crown on, **jump at a portrait and push up** to warp. Go to the **bottom** (the volcano area where Wings/Fire Rod were);
   get a slime to approach, **jump off it with Jump Boots** to the left, **into the painting**; **hold left**, fall onto the green
   platform, jump in → **Dragon Slayer**. **Crystal out** (don't walk out).
3. House → **Roas**, equip **Dragon Slayer + Jump Boots + Power Boots**. Go to **Keela** at the crossroads; with the Dragon
   Slayer equipped, **walk past the left pillar** → Keela awakens.
4. **Keela** (infinite magic this fight): wait for it to **land at the back of the screen**, then **jump-attack the FACE at
   max range** and keep backing away. **Never attack it in the air** (it fires). After it dies, **do NOT touch the flaming
   bones** (instant death, no win credit). → ending.
